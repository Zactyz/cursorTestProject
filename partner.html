Partnership FAQs

Common questions about partnering with Ciple

What are the benefits of becoming a church partner?

Church partners receive access to congregational analytics, group prayer walls, custom study plans, and a dedicated dashboard for church leadership. You'll also get priority support and the ability to create church-specific content for your members.

Is there a minimum commitment for monthly supporters?

No, there's no minimum commitment for monthly supporters. You can contribute any amount that fits your budget, and you can pause or cancel your support at any time. Every contribution, regardless of size, helps us maintain our commitment to keeping Ciple free for all users.

How does ministry collaboration work?

Ministry collaborations are customized based on your organization's needs. This can include API access to integrate Ciple with your existing systems, co-creating content, or developing specialized features for your specific ministry context. We start with a discovery call to understand your needs, then create a tailored partnership plan.

Can we get custom branding for our church or ministry?

Yes, church and ministry partners at certain levels can access custom branding options. This includes your logo in the app for your members, custom color schemes, and branded study materials. Contact us to discuss the specific branding options available for your partnership level.

How is my donation or partnership contribution used?

Your contributions go directly toward maintaining and improving Ciple. This includes server costs, development of new features, content creation, and expanding our reach to believers worldwide. As a nonprofit organization, we're committed to transparency in how we use funds, and we provide regular updates to our partners on the impact of their support.
