About Ciple

Transforming discipleship through AI-powered personalization while maintaining biblical authenticity.

Our Mission

Making spiritual growth accessible to all

At Ciple, we believe that deep, transformational faith journeys should be accessible to everyone, regardless of financial resources or theological background. Our mission is to leverage AI technology to help people develop a mature, biblically-grounded faith through personalized discipleship resources.

We are committed to keeping Ciple free forever, guided by Jesus' words in Matthew 10:8: "You received without paying; give without pay." Our team of theologians, technologists, and ministry leaders works to ensure all content remains biblically sound while leveraging innovation to meet people wherever they are on their spiritual journey.

Support Our Mission

Our Vision

We envision technology serving as a bridge, not a barrier, to authentic spiritual growth. Ciple aims to be the leading AI-powered discipleship platform that complements—never replaces—personal relationships, church community, and direct Scripture engagement.

By 2025, we aim to serve over 1 million users worldwide, partner with 5,000+ churches, and continue developing adaptive technology that meets believers' unique needs while remaining grounded in biblical truth.
