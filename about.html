About Ciple

We're on a mission to transform discipleship through AI-powered personalization while maintaining biblical authenticity.

Our Mission

Ciple exists to make discipleship accessible, engaging, and personalized for everyone seeking to deepen their faith journey. We believe that by combining innovative technology with biblical truth, we can help people grow in their relationship with Christ in a way that meets them where they are.

Our nonprofit model ensures that spiritual growth resources remain accessible to everyone regardless of financial means. We're guided by Matthew 10:8, "You received without paying; give without pay," as we seek to empower believers worldwide.

Support Our Mission

Our Vision

We envision a world where technology serves as a bridge, not a barrier, to authentic spiritual growth. Ciple aims to be the leading AI-powered discipleship platform that complements—never replaces—personal relationships, church community, and direct engagement with Scripture.

By 2025, we aim to serve over 1 million users worldwide, partner with 5,000+ churches, and continue developing adaptive technology that meets the unique needs of each believer while remaining firmly grounded in biblical truth.
