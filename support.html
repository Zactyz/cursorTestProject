Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about donating and partnering with us

Is my donation tax-deductible?
Yes, Ciple operates as a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization, making all donations tax-deductible for U.S. taxpayers. You will receive a receipt for your tax records after each donation.
How are donations used?
Donations directly support the development and maintenance of Ciple's AI-powered discipleship tools, server costs, theological oversight, outreach to underserved regions, and minimal administrative expenses. We are committed to financial transparency and publish annual reports on our website.
Can I specify how my donation is used?
Yes, you can designate your gift for specific initiatives such as global outreach, technology development, or theological resources. Simply include a note with your donation, and we will honor your preferences.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, bank transfers, and cryptocurrency donations. For other methods or questions about larger gifts, please contact our support team directly.
How can churches partner with Ciple?
Churches can partner with us through financial support, promotional partnerships, beta testing new features, and providing theological feedback. We offer special partnership programs for ministries of all sizes. Contact us for more details.
